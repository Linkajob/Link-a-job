Founder of Link A Job, Janak Panthi, Receives Youth Changemaker Award 2024

Lumbini, Nepal — Archived from 2024 | Published: January 1, 2026

Janak Panthi, founder of the digital employment platform Link A Job, has been officially honored with the Youth Changemaker Award 2024 by the Lumbini Province Youth Council under the Ministry of Social Development. The award recognizes his significant contributions to social innovation and youth empowerment through ethical and accessible recruitment practices.

Panthi’s leadership in tackling youth unemployment through Link A Job was highlighted during the ceremony. The platform, developed in partnership with government stakeholders, removes registration and placement fees for job seekers while promoting transparency and fairness in the recruitment process.

By eliminating financial barriers, Link A Job has expanded access to employment opportunities across Nepal, aligning closely with national goals for inclusive economic growth. Officials praised the initiative as a prime example of effective public–private collaboration driven by youth leadership.

“This recognition reflected the impact that collaborative innovation can create when public vision and private execution work together.” — Janak Panthi

At the award event, youth leaders, policymakers, and social innovators gathered to acknowledge the growing role of youth-led digital platforms in Nepal’s social and economic development.

About Link A Job: The platform is a digital recruitment ecosystem connecting Nepali talent with employment opportunities in a fair and transparent manner. It operates without charging registration or placement fees, promoting ethical hiring and inclusive workforce development nationwide.
